# Official Contact & Support Page

## Page Goal
Provide a single, official, and secure way for users (especially from Reddit)
to contact me and connect to the app, without using Reddit DMs or exposing
personal contact details.

---

## Page Type
- Single-page contact hub
- Mobile-first
- No login required
- Minimal interaction

---
